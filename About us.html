About Us

The Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) program at the University of Nairobi is designed to prepare students for careers in the pharmaceutical and healthcare sectors. Our rigorous curriculum combines theoretical knowledge with practical laboratory and clinical training, ensuring graduates are well-equipped to meet the evolving needs of the health industry.

We pride ourselves on a dedicated faculty of experts and state-of-the-art facilities that foster innovation, research, and community engagement. Our students gain hands-on experience in drug formulation, patient care, pharmacology, and pharmacy practice.

As part of East Africa's leading university, the Bachelor of Pharmacy program emphasizes ethical practice, professionalism, and lifelong learning, empowering our graduates to contribute significantly to national and global health.

We welcome prospective students who are passionate about making a difference in healthcare and invite you to join us as we shape the future of pharmacy.
